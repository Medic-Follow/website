SEPT 2020

Foundation of MedicFollow

The MedicFollow project was founded during the MoonShot 2020 Pool at Epitech Marseille.

OCTO 2020

Presentation of MedicFollow at "The M Accelerator"

MedicFollow, having been elected the best EIP project at Epitech Marseille, has been chosen to be presented to "The M Accelerator", an association specializing in helping and supporting young companies in the area.

DECE 2020

Determination of objectives for the closed beta

The first "BRAINSTORMING" sprint allowed MedicFollow to develop new ambitions and new goals for the future of development.

JANU 2021

MedicFollow Hospital/Medical Survey

Collection of opinions from medical professionals concerning the need for a tool to facilitate communication between relatives and the staff caring for the patient(s).

MARC 2021

Start of Closed Beta ; creation of the "Web" platform for individuals

The first step in the development of the Closed Beta was the creation of the "web" platform, accessible to individuals from any internet browser.

APRI 2021

Creation of the "Mobile" platform ; Creation of "Back-End" services

After the "web" platform, our second objective was to offer MedicFollow solutions for individuals on "Mobile" as well. Background services for the correct execution of MedicFollow functionality have begun to be developed.

JULL 2021

Addition of a solution for professionals to the "Web" platform

The main objective being to save time for our medical staff, our "web" platform has received a content update to allow professionals to use our services more easily than individuals.

SEPT 2021

Added "Discussion" functionality to both MedicFollow platforms

The main asset for facilitating communication between medical staff and relatives: setting up the chat service.

OCTO 2021

Added "Agenda" functionality to both MedicFollow platforms

The secondary asset to bring convenience to our users' daily lives: implementation of the calendar service and smart reminders.

DECE 2021

"Plan-Beta" version of MedicFollow under development

After new feedback from medical staff following our first MedicFollow beta tests, we continue to improve our features.

MAY 2021

MedicFollow "Beta" ready for deployment in medical settings

Our constant improvements in terms of security and wealth of features allow us now to provide a suite of software suitable for the public for testing purposes.
